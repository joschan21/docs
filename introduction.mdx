Welcome to the Splitter documentation! Splitter allows you to set up privacy-friendly A/B-tests for your Next.js project in under 5 minutes. 

**Possible use-cases are**:
- Increasing newsletter sign-ups
- Increasing the amount of recruiters reaching out from your portfolio
- Increasing conversion rates for your SaaS landing page
- ...many more!
